## TL;DR
- Claude Cowork (inside the Claude desktop app) lets you install "plugins," and the creator treats each plugin as a **department** (marketing, sales, customer service, legal, etc.) and builds smaller **teams** inside it using "skills."
- He customizes the **marketing plugin** to his own brand by pointing Cowork at a folder of brand/company documents and letting it auto-generate the commands, skills, and connectors.
- He builds four working pieces with no employees and no freelancers: a **content repurposing team**, a **research team** (which he automates on a schedule), a **content strategist** that pulls analytics by taking over Chrome, and a **brand manager** (sales side).
- Key vocabulary he defines: **commands** = what you type to trigger a plugin; **connectors** = the apps connected to it; **skills** = the "teams" that hold the instructions to do a task.
- You only need a paid Claude plan to use Cowork, and he says the **$20/month plan** is enough to do everything shown.
