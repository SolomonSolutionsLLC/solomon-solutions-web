## The cross-video plays (what to actually steal)
1. **Finish-line / transformation copy** (Erhart, Chidester): rewrite every product to sell the *result*, not features. Simply Pray sells "you prayed every day and felt connected," not "an app with the ACTS method."
2. **Network-first acquisition** (Feras): with no audience, your first consulting clients + design partners come from your existing contacts — AI-drafted, non-pitchy reconnects you approve.
3. **One channel, master it** (Wendt, SaaS Academy): don't spread thin across platforms; pick one, build the funnel, then expand.
4. **Pick the funnel before the channel; build middle-out** (SaaS Academy bowtie): design the waitlist→email→convert path first.
5. **Repurposing engine** (HubSpot Claude+Canva): one Wisdom Walk pillar → dozens of on-brand posts in minutes. This is your time multiplier.
6. **Research-first AI** (Isenberg/Dickerson): always ground AI in real research/brand voice to avoid generic "slop" — exactly how these tutorials were built.
7. **Skill/orchestrator pattern** (Rogoff, Saunders): turn your Stage-4 agents into real Claude Code skills with compliance + human-approval baked in.
