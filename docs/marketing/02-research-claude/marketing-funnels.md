# Modern marketing funnels

> A marketing funnel is the map of the journey a stranger takes from "never heard of you" to "paying customer who tells friends" — and the deliberate steps you put in place to help them move along it.

## Beginner explanation

Imagine a kitchen funnel: wide at the top, narrow at the bottom. Lots of people pour in at the top (they discover you), and fewer come out the bottom (they buy). The "funnel" just pictures that drop-off so you can see where people get stuck.

People do not go from stranger to customer in one leap. They move through stages. Here are the five, in plain English:

1. **Awareness** — they first find out you exist (a Google search, an Instagram reel, a friend's recommendation).
2. **Capture** — you get permission to keep talking to them. Usually that means they hand over an email address. This is where a **lead magnet** comes in: a free, useful thing (a PDF guide, a checklist, a mini-course) you give away in exchange for their email. A **lead** is simply a person who has raised their hand and given you a way to reach them.
3. **Nurture** — you build trust over time, usually by email, by being genuinely helpful before you ask for anything.
4. **Conversion** — they take the action you want: buy, subscribe, or book a call.
5. **Retention & referral** — they stay, keep paying, and tell others.

A few terms you will see everywhere:

- **TOFU / MOFU / BOFU** = Top, Middle, and Bottom Of Funnel. TOFU is awareness content for people just realizing they have a problem. MOFU is for people comparing solutions. BOFU is for people ready to buy and just need a nudge ([NotScheduled, 2025](https://www.notscheduled.com/blog/tofu-mofu-and-bofu-the-best-saas-content-marketing-funnel-explained)).
- **CTA** = Call To Action. The specific next step you ask for: "Download the guide," "Book a free call," "Start free."
- **Conversion rate** = the percentage of people who took the desired action. If 100 people visit and 3 book a call, that is a 3% conversion rate ([VWO, Apr 2026](https://vwo.com/blog/what-is-a-good-funnel-conversion-rate/)).

## Why it matters

Without a funnel, you are doing random acts of marketing: a post here, a webpage there, hoping something sticks. A funnel turns marketing into a system you can measure and fix. When you see that 1,000 people read your article but only 5 joined your email list, you know exactly which step is leaking.

The numbers are sobering and freeing at once. Most sales funnels convert only 3–10%, median around 6.6% across industries ([VWO, Apr 2026](https://vwo.com/blog/what-is-a-good-funnel-conversion-rate/)). Most visitors never buy, and that is normal. The goal is not 100%; it is steady improvement at each stage. A funnel also protects your most precious asset in a trust-driven niche: the relationship. It lets you earn trust *before* you ask for money, which matters enormously in ministry.

## How it applies to YOUR products

You have one budget (~$1,000/mo), little time (2–5 hrs/wk), no audience yet, and an organic-first plan. So build **one shared top of funnel** that feeds every product, rather than five separate funnels you cannot maintain.

**The Wisdom Walk is your funnel's mouth.** It is the awareness + trust hub. Helpful, genuinely Christian articles answer the questions your four personas already type into Google. Every article ends with a CTA to capture an email (a lead magnet). This is the cheap, organic engine that feeds everyone else, so it gets the lion's share of your few hours.

Then each product is a different *bottom* of the funnel:

- **Simply Pray (priority, pre-launch):** This is freemium, so the funnel is a **waitlist** right now. Lead magnet idea: a free printable "ACTS Prayer Guide" on The Wisdom Walk. CTA: "Join the Simply Pray early-access list." Nurture by email until launch. Expect freemium free-to-paid conversion around 3–5% once live — traditional freemium averages about 3.7% ([First Page Sage, Jun 2026](https://firstpagesage.com/seo-blog/saas-freemium-conversion-rates/)), so a big free top-of-funnel matters. Reaches Devout Dana and, through the $49/mo Church tier, Pastor Paul.
- **Solomon Solutions consulting (high-ticket B2B):** Classic consultant funnel. BOFU offer is the free 30-min strategy call. B2B converts lower and slower (early stage visitor-to-lead around 2–5%) ([VWO, Apr 2026](https://vwo.com/blog/what-is-a-good-funnel-conversion-rate/)), but one closed church client is worth a lot, so this is where paid ads eventually pay off.
- **HopeStack (B2B SaaS, beta + waitlist):** Highest revenue-per-customer, narrow audience (Care Director Carol). Funnel = targeted BOFU content + demo/waitlist signup. Tightly targeted SaaS can convert 5–15%, far above broad tools ([daydream](https://www.withdaydream.com/library/insights/freemium-conversion-rate)). Few leads needed; quality over volume.
- **Heartward (low-ARPU consumer):** Reaches Seeker Sam and Dana. Funnel = a Q&A article or free trial of the AI, then a paid tier. Volume game; let The Wisdom Walk feed it cheaply.

Organic-first sequence: prove the message free on The Wisdom Walk, watch which articles capture the most emails, *then* spend the $1,000 amplifying only what already works.

## Examples

**Freemium app (Simply Pray):** Pastor Paul searches "how to teach my church to pray" → lands on a Wisdom Walk article on the ACTS method → downloads the free ACTS Prayer Guide (capture) → gets a 4-email nurture sequence ("Why structure helps prayer," a testimony, a teaching, then the offer) → joins the early-access waitlist → at launch, starts free, upgrades to the $49/mo Church tier (conversion) → renews and recommends it to a pastor friend (retention/referral).

**B2B SaaS (HopeStack):** Care Director Carol reads a Wisdom Walk piece on "HIPAA-aligned record-keeping for church counseling centers" → clicks a BOFU CTA "See how HopeStack handles HIPAA" → books a demo → joins the beta → becomes a paying center.

**Consultant (Solomon Solutions):** A lead pastor reads "Should your church build or buy AI?" → CTA "Book a free 30-min strategy call" → call → proposal → engagement.

## Common mistakes

- **Building five funnels at once.** With 2–5 hrs/week you cannot. Run one shared TOFU (The Wisdom Walk) feeding all products.
- **No capture step.** Driving traffic with no email signup means you rebuild your audience from scratch every month. Always trade a lead magnet for an email.
- **Asking for the sale too early.** In a trust-driven faith niche this backfires fastest. Nurture first.
- **Email that feels like noise.** Faith audiences unsubscribe quickly from filler; every email needs a real reason to exist — a devotional, a genuine update, a real invitation ([Church Brand Guide, 2025](https://churchbrandguide.com/use-email-marketing-to-grow/)).
- **Spending on ads before the message is proven.** Paying to send traffic into a funnel that does not convert organically just burns your $1,000 faster.
- **Compliance blind spots.** HopeStack is HIPAA-aligned; never use real client stories or data in marketing without explicit, documented consent.
- **Chasing one magic conversion number.** Compare each stage to itself over time, not to a single industry average ([VWO, Apr 2026](https://vwo.com/blog/what-is-a-good-funnel-conversion-rate/)).

## Recommended tools

| Tool | Job in the funnel | Rough cost/mo | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| The Wisdom Walk (your site) | TOFU content/SEO hub | already owned | Your free awareness engine |
| MailerLite or Beehiiv | Capture + nurture (email) | $0–10 | Generous free tiers; cheap |
| ConvertKit/Kit | Email if you outgrow above | ~$0–25 | Free up to 10k subs |
| Google Search Console | See what people search to find you | free | Essential, free |
| Plausible or GA4 | Measure funnel drop-off | $0–9 | GA4 free; Plausible simpler/paid |
| Calendly | Book the consulting strategy call | $0–12 | Free tier works |
| Canva | Make lead magnets & social images | $0–15 | Free tier is plenty |
| Meta/Google Ads | Paid amplification (later) | from your $1k | Only after organic proof |

Most of a real starter stack here can be free or under ~$50/mo, leaving your $1,000 for ads once the message is proven.

## First 3 actions for Joshua

1. **Pick one lead magnet and one product to start.** Make it the free "ACTS Prayer Guide" feeding the Simply Pray waitlist (your priority). Have AI draft it; you approve. (~1–2 hrs)
2. **Set up free email capture.** Create a MailerLite or Beehiiv account, build one signup form and one "thank you" page, and embed the form on the relevant Wisdom Walk article. (~1 hr)
3. **Write one TOFU article + one CTA.** Publish (or AI-draft for your approval) one genuinely helpful Wisdom Walk article that answers a real question a persona Googles, ending in a clear CTA to grab the lead magnet. Then watch the signup rate. (~1–2 hrs)
