## The golden threads (what showed up across every topic)
These recurring findings ARE your strategy:

1. **Email is your highest-ROI move — and faith has an unfair advantage** (religion has among the highest email open rates of any industry). You have no list. Fixing this is move #1. Tool: **MailerLite free** (≤1,000 subs).
2. **The Wisdom Walk is the engine** — SEO surface, AI-search citation source, pillar for repurposing, email magnet. Connecting it to the funnel is the single highest-leverage structural fix.
3. **Prove the message free, then pay** — validation (#12) + organic (#2) before paid (#3). $1k/mo can't afford to learn via ad spend.
4. **AI search is reshaping discovery in 2026** — Google AI Overviews cut organic clicks materially. Being the *cited answer* (AEO/GEO, #5) now matters as much as ranking. Your Reformed/theological depth is a real moat.
5. **Single-page hash-anchor sites have a structural SEO problem** — split into real pages (features, pricing, church-plan, articles) so search/AI can find you.
6. **Faith content gets throttled on paid ad platforms** — real cases of Google/TikTok rejecting Christian-app ads. Another reason organic-first is right; write benefit-led (not preachy) ad copy when you do pay.
7. **Pastors are adopting AI fast** — tailwind for your consulting (private LLM deployment) and Heartward.
