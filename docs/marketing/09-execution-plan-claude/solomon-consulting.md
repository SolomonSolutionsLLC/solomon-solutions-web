## 1. The 3 highest-leverage plays

**Play 1 — Network-first outreach (your fastest path to client #1).** With no audience, the first clients come from Joshua's *existing* contacts — former colleagues, denominational connections, seminary friends, friends-of-friends. Religion has a **55.7% email open rate vs ~43% overall**, and one-to-one notes beat any blast. This is where the first dollars come from in 30 days. *From the [Feras consulting tutorial](../03-youtube-tutorials-claude/ultimate-guide-starting-consulting-business-2026.md) + [consulting funnel, channel #1](../05-funnels-claude/solomon-consulting-funnel.md); open rate [MailerLite 2025](https://www.mailerlite.com/blog/compare-your-email-performance-metrics-industry-benchmarks).*

**Play 2 — Productize three tiers behind the free call.** The call is the conversion event; the tiers are what gets sold (tiers let the buyer self-select and make the middle feel safe): **Tier 1 "Clarity Audit"** (fixed-fee diagnostic + roadmap, 2–3 wks) → **Tier 2 "Systems & Discipleship Build"** (multi-month) → **Tier 3 "Private AI for Ministry"** (private LLM on the church's own VPS — the differentiator almost no other consultant can deliver). *From [consulting funnel, core offer](../05-funnels-claude/solomon-consulting-funnel.md) + the [Jason Whaling pricing tutorial](../03-youtube-tutorials-claude/how-much-to-charge-consulting-pricing-strategy.md) (3P recurring stack).*

**Play 3 — "Sell the finish-line result," lead with the AI-anxiety topic.** Pastors are adopting AI fast and anxiously, and nobody's giving them a *pastoral, ethics-first* answer (everyone else sells tools). The "Pastor's Guide to AI" magnet positions Joshua as the trustworthy guide and teases Tier 3. *Finish-line copy from the [Adam Erhart service-business tutorial](../03-youtube-tutorials-claude/marketing-sales-strategy-for-service-based-business.md); AI-adoption tailwind from [research golden threads](../02-research-claude/00-research-index-claude.md).*

## 3. The decided specifics

- **Lead magnet:** "The Pastor's Guide to AI: 7 Honest Questions Before You Use It in Ministry" (PDF, ~6–8 pp). Secondary: "The Healthy Church Systems Scorecard" (self-scored assessment → pre-sells Tier 1).
- **Landing page:** single job = book the free 30-min call. Hero: **"Built by a pastor, not a vendor"** (sub: "Church systems, leadership, and AI — guided by someone who's stood in your pulpit"). Booking form ≤5 fields (name, email, church, biggest challenge). Blocks: empathy ("You didn't get into ministry to manage software") → 3 help cards → "why a pastor not a vendor" founder block → "what a call looks like" (no pitch, no pressure) → magnet capture for the not-ready → repeat CTA.
- **Welcome sequence (5 emails):** (1) here's your guide + why I made it, (2) the AI question pastors are afraid to ask, (3) what "good systems" actually look like, (4) how I work with churches (no pressure), (5) the ask — book the 30-min call. Then drop to the weekly Wisdom Walk newsletter.
- **Channels (exactly two):** (1) warm direct outreach (the revenue channel); (2) long-form Wisdom Walk content repurposed to **LinkedIn** (the compounding channel — pastors behave professionally there, faith content less throttled). *Not* Instagram/TikTok/paid yet.
- **Brand:** Navy `#16213e` · gold `#c9a84c` · cream `#faf7f0`. Confident, sober, anti-hype. ([brand system](../08-brand-kits-claude/00-brand-system-claude.md))

## 5. Compliance guardrails (what NOT to say)
- **No fear, guilt, manipulation** ("your church is failing if…" ❌), no false scarcity, no countdown pressure.
- **Never "vendor" language** — lead with ministry outcomes, not features/"solutions packages."
- **Don't promise spiritual or numerical results you can't control** — no "we'll grow your church," no guaranteed conversions/baptisms. Promise clarity, structure, wise tools; God grows the church.
- **Handle AI honestly** — never imply AI replaces the pastor, the Spirit, or genuine discipleship; privacy/ethics caveats front and center (that honesty *is* the selling point). Don't encourage pasting sensitive pastoral/counseling info into public AI tools — that's why Tier 3 exists.
- **Scripture with care, not decoration; non-partisan/non-divisive; no fake testimonials** until you have real ones.
