# The Wisdom Walk — Execution Plan
**The always-on engine under everything else (not a "launch").** Part of [Stage 9](00-master-plan.md). Source funnel: [05-funnels-claude/wisdom-walk-funnel.md](../05-funnels-claude/wisdom-walk-funnel.md).

> The Wisdom Walk sells nothing directly. Its job: **attract** the right people with articles, **capture** their email, **nurture** trust, then **route** them to the product that fits (Simply Pray / HopeStack / Heartward / consulting). It's the church foyer — nobody gets saved in the foyer, but everybody walks through it. This is the single highest-leverage *structural* fix in the whole engagement ([audit §6](../01-intake-and-audit-claude.md); [research golden thread #2](../02-research-claude/00-research-index-claude.md)).

## Personas
- **Pastor Paul (primary).** Highest-value node — one Paul who trusts you can buy consulting, put his church on Simply Pray, *and* champion HopeStack. Searches for sermon help, discipleship frameworks, "how do I think biblically about ___."
- **Care Director Carol (secondary).** Reads the biblical-counseling content; converts to HopeStack fast. (Dana → Simply Pray/Heartward and Sam → Heartward are quietly served by the same capture + routing.)

## 1. The 3 highest-leverage plays

**Play 1 — One pillar article a week → repurpose into everything (the time multiplier).** Write once, slice many: one 1,200–1,800-word article becomes a newsletter + 3 LinkedIn posts + lead-magnet fuel. This is how 2–5 hrs produces a full week of presence across the ecosystem. AI drafts, Joshua edits for theology/voice. *From [content-repurposing.md](../02-research-claude/content-repurposing.md) + the [HubSpot Claude+Canva tutorial](../03-youtube-tutorials-claude/32-social-posts-10-min-claude-canva.md) (32 posts in 10 min) — run via `marketing-repurposer`.*

**Play 2 — Own the email channel (faith's unfair advantage).** Religion has the **highest open rate of any industry — 55.71% in 2025, #1 measured**. Email is the one channel no algorithm can throttle, and the lead magnets are the bait that turns an anonymous reader into a known, routable subscriber. The newsletter ("The Walk") + 5-email welcome sequence is the relationship engine. *From [email-capture-nurture.md](../02-research-claude/email-capture-nurture.md); [MailerLite 2025](https://www.mailerlite.com/blog/compare-your-email-performance-metrics-industry-benchmarks).*

**Play 3 — Be the cited answer (GEO/AEO) on the questions pastors actually ask.** AI Overviews cut organic clicks ~35–60%, but brands cited *inside* AI answers earn ~35% more clicks. A real, named pastor-engineer author + precise Scripture + question-shaped H2s + FAQ schema is exactly what AI quotes — and the "30 Hard Questions" magnet doubles as AI-bait (publish gated PDF + ungated web Q&A pages). *From [wisdom-walk funnel, AI-search section](../05-funnels-claude/wisdom-walk-funnel.md) + [seo.md](../02-research-claude/seo.md) + [ai-search-aeo-geo.md](../02-research-claude/ai-search-aeo-geo.md).*

## 3. The decided specifics

- **Lead magnets (staged):** #1 flagship **"The Pastor's Discipleship Pathway"** (printable one-page framework + 5-page guide) — launch with it. #2 by day 30 **"30 Hard Questions Your People Are Asking — and Biblical Answers"** (serves Paul + Carol, prime AI-citation format, publish gated + ungated). #3 by day 60 **"The Quiet Pastor's Toolkit"** (7-day email mini-course → trains daily opens, introduces Simply Pray).
- **Core offer:** the newsletter "The Walk" — one short Scripture-rooted, genuinely useful email a week. No price; trade = email for wisdom.
- **Landing page:** single job = email for a lead magnet + newsletter. Hero options: A "Biblical wisdom for the weight you carry" · B "For pastors and leaders who want depth, not noise" · C "Sound thinking on discipleship, counseling, and culture — sent to your inbox" · D "Built by a pastor, not a vendor." Button: **"Send me the free guide"** (not "Subscribe"). Blocks: promise (one email/week, Scripture first, no fluff/guilt) → "why me" founder block → sample-quality proof → repeat CTA.
- **Welcome sequence (5 emails):** (1) deliver Pathway + hello, (2) "why a pastor learned to code" (story), (3) pure value, no ask, (4) soft route to a product *as help* (Simply Pray for prayer / HopeStack for care leaders — match the magnet), (5) "what are you walking through?" reply ask (segments by persona for routing).
- **Channels (exactly two):** (1) SEO/AI-search articles (the engine, majority of time); (2) **LinkedIn** as the single distribution amplifier (thoughtful text, reaches Paul + Carol, less throttled than Meta). One social channel only. Paid deferred.
- **Three content pillars (rotate weekly):** 1) Discipleship (→ Simply Pray), 2) Biblical counseling & care (→ HopeStack; Sam/Dana → Heartward), 3) Faith & culture / tech & AI (→ consulting + brand authority). This matches the [unified plan's monthly pillar calendar](../06-unified-marketing-plan-claude.md).
- **Brand:** Ink `#1a1a2e` · gold `#c9a84c` · cream `#faf7f0`. Editorial, wise, pastoral. ([brand system](../08-brand-kits-claude/00-brand-system-claude.md))

## 5. Compliance guardrails (what NOT to say)
- **No manipulation/guilt/fear-based copy**, no "real Christians read this," no false urgency.
- **No prosperity/"give to get" framing** — don't tie spiritual outcomes to clicking/subscribing/buying.
- **No false or inflated proof** — until you have real numbers/testimonials, lead with sample quality, never "join 10,000 pastors."
- **No medical/legal/clinical-counseling promises** — biblical-care content adds a gentle "not a substitute for professional care" note where appropriate.
- **Non-partisan, non-combative** on culture topics ("depth, not noise").
- **Honor consent/privacy** — clear unsubscribe, no bought lists, accurate "what you'll get," real privacy policy.
- **Theological fidelity is the floor** — this is the hub the whole ecosystem leans on; every AI draft gets a pastor's eyes before it ships. Stay anti-vendor in tone ("a pastor who happens to build tools").
