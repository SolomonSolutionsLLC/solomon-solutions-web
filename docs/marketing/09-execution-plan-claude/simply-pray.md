**Play 1 — Fix the dead CTA → waitlist capture (do first, worth more than any ad).**
Replace the broken "Download free" button on simplypray.io with a single-field waitlist form ("Be first to pray with Simply Pray — get the ACTS Guide today"). A dead button converts at 0%; a warm faith waitlist page can sit at the high end of 8–15%. *From [simply-pray-funnel.md, "Urgent fix"](../05-funnels-claude/simply-pray-funnel.md) + [landing-page-conversion.md](../02-research-claude/landing-page-conversion.md).*

**Play 2 — Lead with the ACTS *method*, not the app.** The 500-year-old Adoration-Confession-Thanksgiving-Supplication framework is the product's spine and its #1 marketing asset — "people trust a method; they're suspicious of an app." The "7-Day ACTS Prayer Guide" lead magnet *is* the app's core value delivered free, so downloaders are pre-qualified to love the app, and pastors hand the printable out (organic distribution into churches). *From [simply-pray-funnel.md, lead magnet #1](../05-funnels-claude/simply-pray-funnel.md); finish-line/transformation framing from the [Adam Erhart tutorial](../03-youtube-tutorials-claude/marketing-sales-strategy-for-service-based-business.md).*

**Play 3 — Pastor-to-pastor outreach for the Church plan.** Joshua's single most valuable channel for $49/mo accounts is *ministry conversation* with other pastors — his network, ministerial associations, denominational groups, pastor Facebook groups. Bypasses paid-ad throttling entirely. "Network-first acquisition" *from the [Feras consulting tutorial](../03-youtube-tutorials-claude/ultimate-guide-starting-consulting-business-2026.md) + [simply-pray-funnel.md, channel #2](../05-funnels-claude/simply-pray-funnel.md).*

## 3. The decided specifics

- **Lead magnet:** "The 7-Day ACTS Prayer Guide" — elegant 1-page printable PDF + 7-day email mini-series (one ACTS step/day). Secondary (Month 2): "Pray Together: A Pastor's Toolkit for a Praying Church" (the Pastor Paul magnet).
- **Landing page:** single job = capture one email. Hero options to A/B test — A (method-led): *"Learn to pray — one honest step at a time."* · B (pain-led): *"Ever sit down to pray and run out of words?"* · C (urgency): *"Be among the first to pray with Simply Pray."* CTA: "Reserve my spot" / "Send me the free guide." Mobile-first, <3s load, ≥44px touch targets. Distinct "For churches" band → Church plan / Calendly.
- **Welcome sequence (5 emails):** (1) deliver the guide + Joshua's intro, (2) Adoration+Confession, (3) Thanksgiving+Supplication + founder story, (4) "for you or your whole church?" soft segmentation, (5) waitlist ask + share. *Full copy: [07-campaign-assets/simply-pray-welcome-sequence.md](../07-campaign-assets-claude/simply-pray-welcome-sequence.md).*
- **Channels (exactly two):** (1) SEO + Wisdom Walk content hub; (2) warm pastor-to-pastor outreach. *Not* social-first, *not* paid.
- **Brand:** Green `#2f5c51` (primary) · gold `#b8935a` · bg `#f8f4ef`. DM Sans; reverent, distraction-free, **no streak-guilt**. CTAs "Join the waitlist" / "Get early access." ([brand system](../08-brand-kits-claude/00-brand-system-claude.md))

## 5. Compliance guardrails (what NOT to say)
- **No guilt/shame/fear.** "You're not bad at prayer" ✅ — "Real Christians pray daily" ❌.
- **No prosperity/transactional promises** — prayer is relationship, not a vending machine.
- **No theological overreach or denominational tribalism** — ACTS spans traditions.
- **No fake urgency/scarcity/social proof.** "Be among the first" is honest (pre-launch); "only 3 spots left" is not.
- **Never claim it's on the App Store until it is** — staged, future-tense language only.
- **Handle congregation/member data with explicit care** (Church plan = member management) — privacy clarity is a selling point.
- **Use Scripture accurately and in context** (e.g. Romans 8:26 on the Spirit interceding when we lack words).
