## 1. Executive summary

You have one **faith-tech ecosystem** with a rare, credible wedge — *"built by a pastor, engineered for the church"* — and a founder (pastor + engineer) who is himself the most marketable asset. The products are good and the copy is strong. **The entire bottleneck is the top of the funnel:** no email list, no social presence, no lead magnets, zero social proof, and thin SEO. Nothing captures or follows up with the people who visit.

So the plan is not "do more marketing." It's: **build one capture-and-nurture engine once (The Wisdom Walk + an email list), feed it with one article a week that AI repurposes into everything else, and point it at one priority product at a time — starting with Simply Pray's waitlist and your warmest revenue (pastor-to-pastor + consulting).** Prove messages organically; spend the ~$1k only where organic already works.

**The 3 moves that matter most, in order:**
1. **Stop the bleeding:** put email capture on every site + fix the broken Simply Pray "Download" link → make it a waitlist. *(Week 1)*
2. **Build the engine:** MailerLite + one lead magnet + a 5-email welcome + The Wisdom Walk as the SEO/AI-search hub. *(Weeks 1–3)*
3. **Turn on warm revenue:** direct, founder-to-founder outreach for the Simply Pray **Church plan ($49/mo)** and **consulting** — your fastest cash, zero ad spend. *(Weeks 2–6)*

## 12. What to avoid (the traps)
- **Spreading across 5 products at once.** One engine, one priority. You'll stall otherwise.
- **Running ads before a message is proven** — and know that **faith content gets throttled** on Google/TikTok (real rejections documented). Organic first, always.
- **Marketing HopeStack with any PHI, client stories, health-outcome claims, or loose HIPAA promises.** Say "HIPAA-aligned," never imply more. This is a legal line, not a style note.
- **Manipulative spiritual urgency / guilt / streak-shaming.** Honest benefit language only — it's also your brand.
- **Building the Heartward funnel before you confirm what Heartward is.** Wasted work otherwise.
- **Leaving the dead App Store link live.** It breaks trust on every Simply Pray visit right now.
- **Chasing vanity metrics.** Followers ≠ subscribers ≠ revenue.
- **Doing it all manually.** Let the AI team draft; you stay the approver and the human voice.
