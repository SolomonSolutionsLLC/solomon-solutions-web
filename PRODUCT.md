# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Pastors, church staff, and ministry leaders evaluating tools and consulting for their church. Context: evaluating on a laptop between meetings, or on a phone after a colleague's referral. Skeptical of vendors; warm to fellow practitioners. Secondary: individual believers arriving for Simply Pray or The Wisdom Walk.

## Product Purpose

Parent-company landing page for Solomon Solutions LLC, a studio that builds ministry software (Simply Pray, HopeStack, HeartwardChat, The Wisdom Walk) and provides systems/AI consulting for churches. The page exists to (1) establish credibility ("built by a pastor, engineered for the church"), (2) route visitors to product sites, and (3) convert consulting leads into a free 30-minute strategy call (Calendly).

## Brand Personality

Wise, grounded, craftsman-like. Three words: dignified, pastoral, engineered. Emotional goals: trust, calm confidence, "these people understand ministry." Never hype, never startup-bro, never sterile-corporate.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages (gradient blobs, hero-metric stats rows, floating UI chips).
- Church-media "trendy" sites (grunge textures, oversized ALL CAPS, conference-hype energy).
- Sterile enterprise vendor sites (stock photos of suits shaking hands).

## Design Principles

1. Practice what we preach: the site itself is evidence the studio builds with excellence.
2. Conviction before decoration: Scripture and founder story are load-bearing, not garnish.
3. One clear next step per fold: the strategy call is the conversion spine.
4. Honest statuses: pre-launch products say so; no fake social proof.
5. Quiet luxury over loudness: navy/gold/cream restraint, serif authority.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA target. Audience skews 35-65; body text must be comfortably readable (16px+). Respect prefers-reduced-motion. Keyboard navigable with visible focus.
